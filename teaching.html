Thesis Offerings

We are offering thesis topics on the Bachelor and Master levels to RWTH students.

Topics may include, but are not limited to, the fields of:

  • Software architecture
  • Model-driven software engineering
  • Business process management / workflow systems
  • Internet of things and cyber-physical systems
  • Large language models and artificial intelligence in and for software architectures
  • Event-driven architectures and systems
  • Edge-Cloud architectures
  • Microservice architectures
  • Software engineering for robots

More specific topics will be defined together with the potential supervisors. If you are interested, please get in touch with the head of the research group.
